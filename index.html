Yeeyan

A language, a world.

Welcome to Yeeyan's GitHub Pages.

Founded in 2006, Yeeyan is the largest translator community site and crowdsourcing translation platform in China. More than 50,000 community translators have contributed nearly 300K translated articles during the past 8 years; the website receives 40,000 unique visitors daily.

Yeeyan Gutenberg Project

Yeeyan Gutenberg Project leverages the power of crowdsourcing to translate books in public domain from different foreign languages into Chinese.

Yeeyan W.E. Library

W.E. Library focuses on introducing books on tech culture to Chinese readers. Translated and published titles include Out of Control by Kevin Kelly, On Intelligence by Jeff Hawkins, etc.
